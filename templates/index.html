BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation that uses your height and weight to estimate whether you're underweight, a healthy weight, overweight, or obese. While BMI can provide a useful guideline, it doesn't differentiate between body fat and muscle. For instance, athletes or individuals with high muscle mass may have a high BMI but still be healthy. It's always good to consider additional factors, like body composition and overall health, alongside your BMI.
